Theoretical Underpinnings

Reggio Emilia’s philosophy is based on social constructivist theories, which define children as social active actors in the construction and determination of their social lives (Edwards & Gandhini, 2018). Bruno Ciari, John Dewey, Jean Piaget, and Lev Vygotsky are the most significant theorists of the Reggio Emilia approach.
